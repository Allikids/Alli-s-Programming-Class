To make sure that you all succeed with this course, one of your fellow students had a great idea and now it is a reality (Thank you Robin Watson!).  

We have created a slack channel for all the student of The Complete Web Developer in 2018. A lot of companies use Slack to communicate across teams, and every job I have had as a developer uses Slack. In the spirit of keeping the course as useful as possible for employment in 2018, we are introducing a slack channel where you can: 

- Introduce yourself 
- Ask the community questions 
- Get help with exercises 
- Meet other students around the world 
- Learn how to answer questions 
- Learn from each other

I am very excited about this but it will only work if everybody helps each other out. I know it isn't for everybody, but being able to ask questions and help others when they have questions is the best way to learn. The Q&A section in the course will still be available and I will still answer every question. However, the Slack channel should give you faster replies from the community and learn another valuable tool of a developer.

I hope you find it useful. To join the group, go to this link here. Once there, you can sign up, and click on channels to explore different topics. 

I'll see you in there, and let's make 2018 awesome!
